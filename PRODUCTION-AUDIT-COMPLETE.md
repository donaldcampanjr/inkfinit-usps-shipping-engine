## Product Positioning

### What It Is

**Inkfinit USPS Shipping Engine** is a production-grade WordPress plugin that provides:

- Real-time USPS shipping rates at checkout
- Complete order lifecycle management (tracking, labels, pickup)
- Intelligent shipping presets and automation
- Modern USPS OAuth v3 API (not legacy)
- Enterprise-class security and performance

### Who It's For

- **E-commerce stores** - Any WooCommerce shop shipping items
- **Subscription boxes** - Automated shipping management
- **Multi-site operations** - Pro/Premium tiers support 5+ sites
- **Enterprise businesses** - Dedicated support, custom development
